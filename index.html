Summary / About Me

Born in Texas, grew up in Winston-Salem, NC. I graduated from Samford University in 2012 after which I've worked business-analyst roles and really enjoyed what I've learned. In every job I've had, I've found myself ending up spending well over 50% of my time building spreadsheets - it's the way I've been able to create the most value regardless of my role, and it's what I've enjoyed the most. In September, I decided to try to take the next step in my enjoyment of designing and building tools / organizing data, by doing a web-development bootcamp. I've really enjoyed everything I've been learning, and want to code for the rest of my life.
