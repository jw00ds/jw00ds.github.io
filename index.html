What I do for fun



Shortcuts

I really enjoy learning and using keyboard shortcuts

Here are some of my favorites that I use often:

General Shortcuts
ctrl+arrow key jump between adjacent blocks of text/content/cells
ctrl+shift+arrow key jump between adjacent blocks of text/content/cells, highlighting as you go
tab move to next option/input
shift+tab move to previous option/input
alt+tab toggle active window
ctrl+Z / ctrl+Y lifesavers...
Win+arrow keys split screen windows / move selected window
ctrl+pg up/pg dn cycle between open tabs (I use this most in Excel and Chrome, but also elsewhere)
ctrl+W close window/tab
ctrl+5 format text strikethrough
shift+Win+S begin snip/screengrab
ctrl+P print options
ctrl+F find text
ctrl+H find/replace dialog box
ctrl+home jump cursor to beginning of text

Excel / Google Sheets
ctrl+spacebar select column
shift+spacebar select row
alt , A , G , G group columns or rows
ctrl+1 general options
ctrl+D fill down into selected cells/next
ctrl+R fill down into selected cells/next
alt , A , M remove duplicates
alt , E , S paste special dialog options (usually I use this to navigate to transpose options)
alt , E , S , V paste values
alt , E , S , F paste formulas
alt , E , S , T paste format
F4 toggle formula cell-anchoring when cursor is within / next to a cell reference
alt , F , A save as
ctrl+shift++ [when a row or column is infinitely selected] insert row or col
ctrl+- [when a row or column is infinitely selected] delete row or col
ctrl+shift+- remove formatting
alt , H , O , R rename sheet/tab
ctrl+shift+L filter selected
alt , O , C , W set column width in pixels
alt , H , O , H set row height in pixels
alt , H , O , I autofit column width
ctrl+~ show all formulas
shift+F11 insert new sheet in workbook
ctrl+[ jump to formula's immediate precedent
ctrl+{ jump to formula's precedents (all)
ctrl+] jump to formula's immediate dependent
ctrl+} jump to formula's dependents (all)

Outlook
ctrl+N new email
ctrl+R reply
ctrl+shift+R reply all
ctrl+F forward
ctrl+enter send

VS Code
ctrl+/ toggle a line as commented
alt+up/down arrow keys move selected line up/down
ctrl+L highlight line
shift+alt+F format
alt+*click* create/place multiple cursors
ctrl+D highlight and modify subsequent instance(s) of selection
ctrl+K+O open workbook
ctrl+shift++ zoom in
ctrl+shift+- zoom out
ctrl+B toggle sidebar
ctrl+shift+F search in all files
shift+alt+down arrow key copy/fill down to next line(s)
